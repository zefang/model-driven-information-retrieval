[B] FULL/ Watson Pharma's Oper Chief Wilkinson resigns --Watson Pharma's President Chao to assume oper chief duties
March 23, 2001

 

New York, March 22 (BridgeNews) - Watson Pharmaceuticals' chief operating officer, Fred Wilkinson, resigned from his position to become chief executive and president of Columbia Laboratories. Watson's chairman, chief executive and president, Allen Chao, will assume Wilkinson's duties until a replacement is found.

--Nicole Graves, BridgeNews

Wilkinson Resigns From Watson

CORONA, Calif., March 22 -- WATSON PHARMACEUTICALS, INC. (NYSE: WPI) ANNOUNCED TODAY THAT FRED WILKINSON, CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER AND SENIOR VICE PRESIDENT OF SALES AND MARKETING, HAS RESIGNED FROM WATSON TO BECOME CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER AND PRESIDENT OF COLUMBIA LABORATORIES (AMEX: COB). ON AN INTERIM BASIS, OPERATIONS AND SALES AND MARKETING WILL REPORT DIRECTLY TO DR. ALLEN CHAO, WATSON'S CHAIRMAN, CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER AND PRESIDENT.

"We have a solid and experienced management team in both operations and in our branded and generic sales and marketing divisions. Although we will miss Fred, we wish him well in his new position," said Dr. Chao.
